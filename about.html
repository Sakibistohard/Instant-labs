Learn More About Instant Lab

Instant Lab is an innovative platform that allows users to experiment with chemical reactions safely and instantly. By leveraging modern AI, it provides balanced and unbalanced equations, molecular weights, and reaction conditions for various chemical processes.

Features

  • Generate chemical reactions in real time.
  • View balanced and unbalanced equations.
  • Get molecular weights of reactants and products.
  • Understand reaction conditions, including temperature and type.
